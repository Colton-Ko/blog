# Top Tech Trends to Watch in 2025

The technology landscape is evolving at a breakneck pace. Here are the key trends defining 2025.

## Artificial Intelligence Everywhere

AI is no longer just a buzzword; it's integrated into every aspect of our lives.

### Generative AI 2.0

The next generation of generative AI models can create hyper-realistic video and audio in real-time.

### AI in Healthcare

AI algorithms are now diagnosing diseases with greater accuracy than human doctors.

## Quantum Computing

Quantum computers are finally moving from the lab to real-world applications.

### Cryptography

Post-quantum cryptography is becoming essential to secure data against quantum attacks.

### Drug Discovery

Quantum simulations are accelerating the discovery of new life-saving drugs.

## Sustainable Tech

Green technology is taking center stage as the world fights climate change.

### Solid State Batteries

EVs are becoming cheaper and longer-lasting thanks to solid-state battery technology.

### Carbon Capture

New technologies are making it efficient to capture carbon directly from the atmosphere.
